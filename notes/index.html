Notes

On this page, I set to combine my experiment with Zettelkasten with my open-source philosophy. Starting from August 1st, 2021, I make my way into notes that I can hopefully reuse.

I am the writer of these notes, my future-self is the audience. You will undoubtedly disagree with most of these notes or find them hard to read; this is a continuous work in progress. Nevertheless, you mind find your way and benefits, who knows?

Reluctant to new tools and their short lifetime, I start with the minimum, and I’ll see along as requirements emerge. Familiar with Jekyll and Github Page, I build on my website and rely on markdown files. I use wiki links to connect my notes, which I automatically map into backlinks. I list the notes without backlinks below as they are starting points.

I take a lot of inspiration from Niklas Luhmann’ Zettelkasten, Andy Matuschak’ evergreen notes, Sönke Ahrens’ book and the Soren Bjornstad’s TiddlyWiki.
