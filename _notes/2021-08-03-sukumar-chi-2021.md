This paper looks at the issue of compliance, i.e. whether the data collected about participants of a large-scale, longitudinal study misses data points. The authors highlight the lack of knowledge around monitoring and dealing with compliance.

They designed a web-based tool for monitoring compliance. How do they publish this? How do they report findings on such tool?

They first pick and motivate a challenge and implement a tool to address such challenge? Their is no clear evaluation but rather a highlight of the process, refinement of the requirements and details of the design iterations.
