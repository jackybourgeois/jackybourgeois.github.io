[[skatova-plos-one-2019]] point at the interesting, though the disappointing fact that academic researchers cannot use industry data for health research because of the absence of framework.

So data is gold for companies as they buy or collected it in the shadow. It is difficult to access and handle by data subjects (e.g. what would they do with data, lacking technical skills). Researchers could make purposeful use but don't have ways to reach it.
