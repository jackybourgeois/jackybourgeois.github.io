[[steen-sthv-2015]] uses virtue ethics to highlight (implicit) ethics practices at play in a [[participatory-design-pd]] process. Virtue ethics is about cultivating virtues and enabling people to flourish.

He surfaces four virtues for the team members of a Participatory Design process:

* Promoting cooperation, to encourage the participation of all stakeholders
* Cooperative curiosity, to be open and receptive towards other people (mutual learning, ethnography). Steen particularly highlights the challenge to find the line between balance sensitivity and receptiveness.
* Cooperative creativity, to jointly generate ideas
* Empowerment, the disposition to share power and agency with others
