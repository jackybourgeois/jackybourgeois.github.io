Codesign refers to 'collective creativity as it is applied across the whole span of a design process' (Sanders and Stappers 2008, 6)

It is a pragmatic approach to facilitating collaboration and creativity. Its focus is generative activities.

[[steen-sthv-2015]] refers to codesign as 'a process of joint inquiry and imagination, involving perception in problem setting and conception in solution finding.'_
