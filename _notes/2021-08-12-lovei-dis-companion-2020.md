In this paper, Lovei and colleagues provide six concrete tasks for a "design plumber". It is a provocative metaphor to highlight practical but fundamental tasks a design team must do while using a [[data-enabled-design-ded]] approach.

When preparing, designers must configure and deploy design probes. Then, they must maintain and supervise the data collection. Finally, they must handle the data from cleaning to distribution.
