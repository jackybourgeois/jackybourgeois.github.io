Data Donation implies that datasets are dynamic: participants join and leave the project. They donate data already collected or continuously contribute data. Therefore, it raises the need for monitoring the dataset.

It relates to the work of [[sukumar-chi-2021]] that monitor the compliance of participants in a longitudinal study.

[[lovei-dis-companion-2020]] also mention such as "design plumber" task.

How would we conduct a longitudinal study? What type of question do they answer, and how do they report such a study?
