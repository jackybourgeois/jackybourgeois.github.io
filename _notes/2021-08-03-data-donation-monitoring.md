Data Donation implies that datasets are dynamic: participants join and leave the project, and they donate data already collected or continuously donate data. It raises the need for monitoring the dataset.

This relates to the work of [[sukumar-chi-2021]] that monitor compliance of participants in a longitudinal study. 

How would we conduct a longitudinal study? What type of question do they answer, and how do they report such a study?
