Participatory Design comes from a 'Scandinavian' approach to information systems. Its roots are in unions of workers to promote workplace democracy, empowerment, involving the people.

[[steen-sthv-2015]]

_'the people destined to use the system play a critical role in designing it'_ (Schuler and Namioka 1993, xi)

It shifted from political to ethical motives.
